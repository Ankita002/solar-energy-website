WHAT WE DO?
Changing the life of underprivileged people!



WHAT WE DO?
We make it easy for you to help those in need and provide you doorstep service of NGOs. We understand that the smile your donations bring is priceless and offer you Share points as a token of thanks for your step to spread happiness and bring a difference :) You can contact us via sms , call, facebook or just drop your contact details at Contact us section. We will fix appointment with you and send volunteer to collect donations at your doorstep. Now spreading happiness is just a click away!
WHY WE DO?
We wish to help underprivileged people and wants to bring smile on their faces. We can spread happiness by donating old clothes, shoes, books, copies, pen etc but you are not able to do the same due to your inability to take out time and drive to NGO to donate the stuffs every now and then. Hence we end up giving away the stuffs to anyone who may not need it or we just throw the things away.Even if you donate an old pair of shoes, we will show you how it has brought smile on the face of an innocent bud.
HOW WE DO?
As soon as we receive the notification that you want to make some donations, we contact you and fix an appointment as per your convenience. Our Volunteer will come to your place at the appointed time and collect the stuffs. We have the requirement lists from different partner NGOs. We map and segregate the donations according to their requirements.

ABOUT US!

There are many benefits of donating your old stuff through us!

Humanity on Charity is the best way to make non-monetary donations.


Humanity on Charitry is a non-profit startup venture which helps underprivileged people. We support CSR activities of corporates and provides doorstep donation service to individuals/corporate donors. HOC aims at building an environment where individual or corporates believe in social responsibility. We at HOC believe that non monetary donations are the best way to get instant smile on the faces of those who seldom have a reason to smile." Your old bag could help underprivileged child to take their books to school instead of taking in a poly bag. Your old bag can bring smile on the faces of those who seldom have a reason to smile. :)
